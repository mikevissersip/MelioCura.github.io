Products & Services

MelioCura provides both hardware and sofware solutions for physical therapists and hospitals. Mainly focussing on joint rehabilitation after injuries or surgeries. .

"The Shoulder"

Rehabilitation of the shoulder joint after an injury or surgery can be challenging. In-clinic progress is barely tracked, and there is no way to monitor or assist home rehabilitation exercises. Introducing: "The Shoulder" — a device that measures the mobility, strength, and muscle activation of the shoulder joint. The applications are endless: rehabilitation can be carried out from the hospital, clinic, or home, reducing the stress on the healthcare system. "The Shoulder" can even be used for research purposes.

Mobile App

With the integrated mobile platform, physicians maintain full oversight and control over every stage of the rehabilitation process. They are empowered to create and tailor personalized treatment plans based on each patient's unique condition and recovery goals. Patients can then follow these therapy programs conveniently from home, reducing the need for frequent in-clinic visits. Throughout the process, real-time data is collected and securely stored, offering valuable insights into the patient’s progress. Both the physician and patient have access to this data, fostering transparent communication, timely adjustments to the treatment plan, and more effective, data-driven recovery outcomes.
